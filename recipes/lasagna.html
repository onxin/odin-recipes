Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is one of the main Italian dishes, and trust us on this: it's freaking amazing.

Ingredients

  • 100g carbonella
  • 5g zenzero
  • 90g sugar
  • 5 eggs

Steps

  1. Heat up the oven
  2. Put the carbonella in the oven
  3. Mix the eggs with sugar
  4. Throw away the eggs
  5. Go take a walk
  6. Enjoy your warm oven and your sugar!
